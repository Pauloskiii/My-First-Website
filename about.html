about me

Ekpetun Tekenane Paul, born on the 20th of May 1995 is a graduate of Mechanical Engineering with Second Class Upper Honours from the Niger Delta University, Amassoma Bayelsa State Nigeria. After discovering his love for Tech, he made a switch to Information Technology fully in 2022 during his time in the National Service (NYSC).
He is a fast and willng learner with an utmost drive for excellence. He's currently enrolled as a Graduate trainee in the Maiden edition of the Firstbank Technology Academy, a platform for harnessing young Tech Talents, implementing Tech into the Finance sector.
